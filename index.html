FractalEdge – Privacy Policy

Tech2U Savannah · developer.tech2u@gmail.com
Last updated: May 2026  ·  Package: com.fractaledge.app

FractalEdge ("the App") is developed by Tech2U Savannah. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights.

By using FractalEdge, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly from you.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable device identifier used for ad targeting
  • Approximate location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Device information (OS version, device model) for ad optimisation
  • App usage events for ad frequency capping

Ad-related data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only and never uploads it:

  • Lesson progress and quiz scores
  • App preferences and settings
  • Bookmarks and achievements
  • Daily streak data

2. How We Use Information

  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To display your learning progress and achievements within the App
  • To send optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them)

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Billing — optional in-app purchases

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

4. Advertising ID & Opt-Out

You can reset or opt out of personalised ads at any time:

Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalisation

5. Children's Privacy

FractalEdge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact developer.tech2u@gmail.com if you have concerns.

6. Data Retention

All locally stored data remains on your device until you uninstall the App. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data permanently.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored locally on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
